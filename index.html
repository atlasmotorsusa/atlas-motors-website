Questions

Frequently Asked

What areas do you service?

We proudly offer regular coverage throughout Dublin, Pleasanton, Livermore, San Leandro, Castro Valley, San Ramon, Sunol, Fremont, Hayward, Walnut Creek, and Alamo.

Is there an extra fee for long-distance service calls?

Yes. For locations exceeding a 15-mile travel distance from our home hub, we apply a one-time $45 Pavement Extension Premium to account for transit resources and travel time.

Do you supply the parts?

Yes, in most cases we source and bring quality parts to the job. You're also welcome to supply your own parts; if you do, we'll install them but can't warranty the part itself.

How do I pay?

Card, tap-to-pay, or digital payment on-site once the work is complete and you're satisfied. We'll text or email a receipt.

What if the repair needs shop equipment (a lift, alignment rack, etc.)?

Some jobs genuinely need a shop — like frame work or four-wheel alignments. We'll tell you up front if that's the case instead of taking your money for a job we can't finish right.

Is there a diagnostic fee?

Diagnostic pricing is quoted before we arrive and is typically credited toward the repair if you move forward with us.
