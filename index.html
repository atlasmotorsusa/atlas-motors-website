The Fine Print, Made Plain

We Back Our Work

Here's exactly what's covered — no vague promises, no asterisks hiding in the invoice.

✅ Guaranteed by Atlas Motors

  • All initial labor performed on your vehicle is warrantied for a duration of 2 months or 3,000 miles (whichever threshold occurs first).
  • If a repair fails due to workmanship within this window, we will return to diagnose and correct the issue.
  • Subsequent or secondary labor coverage adjustments required after the primary framework period expires are strictly capped at a rate of $70 per hour.

⚙ Covered by the Manufacturer, Not Us

  • Parts are covered only by the part manufacturer's own warranty, not by Atlas Motors.
  • We install customer-supplied parts at the customer's risk if requested.
  • Pre-existing wear, unrelated failures, and normal wear items are not covered.
In short: we guarantee our labor terms. Parts are guaranteed by whoever made them. Full details are listed in our Terms of Service.

Questions

Frequently Asked

What areas do you service?

We come to you within our local service radius. Tell us your address or zip code when booking and we'll confirm coverage — if you're outside the zone we'll let you know before scheduling anything.

What kind of driveway or space do you need?

We just need a relatively flat asphalt or concrete driveway, a dedicated parking space, or a quiet curbside street option where we can safely stand near your vehicle with access for our mobile tool van.

What happens if the weather is bad (heavy rain, storms)?

If harsh outdoor weather conditions create a safety hazard or compromise the quality of the precision mechanical repair, we will proactively reach out to arrange a hassle-free reschedule at your very earliest convenience.

Do I need to be stuck waiting at home during the appointment?

Not at all! That is the beauty of mobile service. As long as you introduce yourself at the start, hand off the keys, and point us to the vehicle, you are free to work inside, run errands, or relax. We will text you a summary when completed.

Do you supply the parts?

Yes, in most cases we source and bring quality parts to the job. You're also welcome to supply your own parts; if you do, we'll install them but can't warranty the part itself.

How do I pay?

Card, tap-to-pay, or digital payment on-site once the work is complete and you're satisfied. We'll text or email a receipt.

What if the repair needs shop equipment (a lift, alignment rack, etc.)?

Some jobs genuinely need a shop — like frame work or four-wheel alignments. We'll tell you up front if that's the case instead of taking your money for a job we can't finish right.

Is there a diagnostic fee?

Diagnostic pricing is quoted before we arrive and is typically credited toward the repair if you move forward with us.
